Welcome To Park Hyatt Jakarta

Situated in Jakarta, 1.3 km from Gambir Station, Park Hyatt Jakarta features accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, private parking, a fitness centre and a garden. Each accommodation at the 5-star hotel has city views, and guests can enjoy access to a terrace and to a sauna. The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, a shuttle service, room service and free WiFi throughout the property. At the hotel rooms are fitted with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a bidet, free toiletries and a hairdryer. At Park Hyatt Jakarta each room has bed linen and towels. The daily breakfast offers Ã  la carte, continental or Italian options. At the accommodation you will find a restaurant serving Indonesian, Italian and Japanese cuisine. Vegetarian, vegan and dairy-free options can also be requested. Park Hyatt Jakarta offers 5-star accommodation with a hot tub. Popular points of interest near the hotel include National Monument, Sarinah and Selamat Datang Monument. The nearest airport is Halim Perdanakusuma International Airport, 24 km from Park Hyatt Jakarta.
